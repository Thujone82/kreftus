Snake VS

Snake VS v1.3: Eat food, grow, & outlast opponents! Modes: VS AI, 2 Player, & 6-Snake Battle Royale (dead snakes become food, rankings at end). Controls: P1 (All Modes): WASD/Arrows or Swipe (2P: bottom half). P2 (2P Mode): Arrows or Swipe top half. Last snake alive wins (or highest rank in Battle)!

GENE

Evolving digital life. Tweak world balance to nudge evolution, see your genomic history.
Controls: Single Tap = Fullscreen Toggle
Double Tap = Play/Pause
Triple Tap = Thanos Event
Pinch/Wheel = Zoom (Paused) / Cell Scale (Running)
Drag (Paused) = Pan View
Swipe (Fullscreen, Running): Vertical = Food Density, Horizontal = Sim Speed
Keyboard: Space = Play/Pause, Arrows = Speed/Food, T = Thanos

Conway's Game of Life

Conway's Game of Life: A cellular automaton where cells live/die based on neighbor counts (2-3 to survive, 3 to be born).
Features: Play/Pause/Step/Reset, Pattern Selector, Wrapped/Infinite Modes, Speed Control, Pan & Zoom (Slider/Mousewheel/Pinch), Click-to-Draw, Save/Restore Grid, Population/Step Counters, Responsive Design, & Colorful Cell Aging.

Double Pendulum

Double Pendulum Simulator: Explore chaotic motion with this interactive double pendulum simulator (v0.7). Adjust the lengths, masses, and starting angles of the pendulum bobs. Customize the simulation's background and the pendulum/trace color; the interface, including sliders with large themed knobs, dynamically adapts to your choices. Watch the physics-based simulation in real-time, see the trace path, and download it as an image. This PWA is installable and offers basic offline use.

SpiroGen

SpiroGen v2.2: Create art with up to 4 nodes, pan/zoom, persistent traces, Node 1 fixed mode & starting angle, PWA support, Memory, and PNG download.
